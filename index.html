Brazil Memory Game

The game starts with 10 "squares" that shows the Brazilian map, once you click on one of the squares a picture of one of my favorites spots in Brazil will show. There will be 5 different beautiful spots total. Once you see the picture you will click on a second picture, the goal is to find the pair of the first picture you clicked on. If both of the pictures are the same you found a match, otherwise the cards will unflip after a few seconds. You have 1 minute to find all 5 pairs. This game will test your memory and hopefully will make you want to visit my country.
